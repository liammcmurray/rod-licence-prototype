Buy a rod fishing licence

You can buy a 1-day, 8-day or 12-month licence online.

You’ll need:

  • a debit or credit card
  • your Blue Badge or National Insurance number, if you have a disability and you’re applying for a 12-month licence
  • the other person’s details (such as date of birth), if you’re buying for someone else

You can use the online service to get a junior licence for children aged 13 to 16. You won’t need to pay.

This licence can start on any date as long as it’s within 90 days of the day you buy it.

Buy now

You can also buy a licence:

  • by calling the Environment Agency
  • in person at a Post Office - if the licence is for someone over 16

Contact the Environment Agency if you haven’t received your 12-month licence within 10 working days.

You can get a 12-month disabled person’s licence if you’ve got a Blue Badge, or you get Disability Living Allowance or Personal Independence Payment (any rate) - you’ll need your National Insurance number to prove this.

You can also get a discounted 12-month licence if you’re over 65. You can’t get a discount for a 1-day or an 8-day licence.

Refunds

In certain circumstances you can be eligible for a refund on a 365 day licence. Please see our full refunds policy for details and contact information.

Extend a licence

To change a 1-day or 8-day licence to a 12-month licence, call the Environment Agency within 14 days of buying it. You’ll need to buy a new licence but you’ll get a refund for the first one you bought.
